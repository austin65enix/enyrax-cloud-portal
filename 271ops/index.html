Safety / Compliance Boundary / 安全與合規邊界

271ops supports readiness preparation and evidence organization. It does not claim ISO27001 certification, legal assurance, or audit approval.

271ops 支援準備度與證據整理，不宣稱取得 ISO27001 認證、法律保證或稽核通過。

  • No secrets.
  • No raw logs by default.
  • No raw prompt / response.
  • Safe references only.
  • Formal decisions remain with management, auditors, consultants, and certified professionals.
