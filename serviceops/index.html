Role-Based Operation View

Infra Member View

Members only see their own tickets, worklog, today's progress, notes and pending reports. This keeps the interface simple and avoids exposing unnecessary team data.

Infra Supervisor View

Supervisors see team workload, member status, total labor hours, blocked tickets and project-linked operation cost.

Executive / Higher-Level View

Higher-level managers can review cross-team capacity, project overrun risk and service delivery trends without entering low-level ticket details.

ProjectOps Connection

Each ticket can be linked to a project, so infra labor time becomes part of real project cost instead of disappearing inside daily operations.

ServiceOps Flow

Step 01
Request
VM, network, storage, account, firewall or system support request enters the queue.
Step 02
Assign
Supervisor assigns owner, priority, due date and project relationship.
Step 03
Worklog
Member records actions, time spent, blockers and completion notes.
Step 04
Cost Map
Labor time is mapped back to project budget, delivery cost and resource usage.
Step 05
Review
Supervisor reviews team workload, risk items and project operation status.
