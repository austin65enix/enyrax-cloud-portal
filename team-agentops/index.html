Safety Boundary / 安全與隱私邊界

Team_AgentOps stores safe metadata only. It does not store full prompts, responses, raw agent sessions, credentials, secrets, API keys, or full home paths.

Team_AgentOps 第一版只保存安全 metadata，不保存完整 prompt、response、raw agent session、credentials、secrets、API keys 或完整 home path。

AI usage records are for governance and protection, not employee surveillance scoring.

AI 使用紀錄的目的不是監控員工，而是保護使用者與團隊，讓 AI 協作有紀錄、有審核、有責任邊界。

Shadow AI Risk / Shadow AI 風險

Risk: Without governance, AI usage can become invisible: no project linkage, no review record, no output traceability, and unclear responsibility boundaries.
如果缺少治理，AI 使用可能變成不可見：沒有專案關聯、沒有審核紀錄、沒有產出追蹤，也沒有清楚的責任邊界。

Team_AgentOps reduces shadow AI risk by linking agent runs to projects, tickets, outputs, reviews, and delivery outcomes.
Team_AgentOps 透過把 Agent 執行紀錄連回專案、工單、產出、審核與交付結果，降低 shadow AI 風險。
